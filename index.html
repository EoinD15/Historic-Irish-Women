Why Learn history with us?

Know Your Roots

Through history, we can learn how past societies, governments, cultures were built, how they operated, and how they have changed which helps us to paint a detailed picture of where we stand today.

Expand Your Mind

History encourages a deeper understanding of difference. There are lessons, both good and bad, to be learned from the way our ancestors have lived.

Past Mistakes

"Those who cannot remember the past are condemned to repeat it" - George Santayana. History helps us to anticipate the future.

Become Inspired

History remembers brilliant people and their heroic acts that have changed the shape of nations.
